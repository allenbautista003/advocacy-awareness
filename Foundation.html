This is the Story of a Civil Rights Movement

The Inclusion BC Foundation is responsible for developing funds to support Inclusion BC and its federation of 75 Community Living agencies across the province. We strengthen the autonomy of inclusion organizations through social enterprise, grants, investment opportunities and cultural capital. Once a year the Foundation asks our friends, allies and new connections to support the movement with a donation. Read this year's story here. For more information on how we're celebrating inclusion as part of Canada's sesquicentennial, visit disabilitypride.ca. - See more at: http://www.inclusionbc.org/our-foundation#sthash.7XyRehBl.dpuf
