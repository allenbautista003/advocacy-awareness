OUR PRIORITIES

We work with children, young people and communities to tackle the root causes of discrimination against girls, exclusion and vulnerability.

CHILD PROTECTION

Enabling children to achieve their full potential and ensuring their overall well-being in a protective and conducive environment.

EDUCATION

We work to promote free, equal access to quality education for all children-from early learning to secondary education.

CHILD PARTICIPATION

Ensuring children and young people to exercise their rights as citizens so they can express their views and influence critical decisions that affect their lives.

ECONOMIC SECURITY

Working to ensure that families in extreme proverty have the skills they need to increase their income and can access financial services.

EMERGENCIES

Working to ensure the relief, recovery, and realisation of rights of girls, boys and caregivers affected by disasters.

HEALTHY START IN LIFE

Working with children, communities and our partners to ensure children's rights to early childhood development are respected, protected and promoted.

SEXUAL REPRODUCTIVE AND HEALTH

Working to ensure children and adolescents realise their rights to sexual and reproductive health and make a safe and healthy transition to adulthood.
